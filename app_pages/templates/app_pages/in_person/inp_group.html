Our Group Class Formats

Choose the learning style that fits your goals, schedule, and preferred class dynamic.

Small Groups (3–6 students)

A focused learning setting offering personalized attention and close interaction while still benefiting from group collaboration.

  • Balanced participation and engagement
  • More individualized instructor feedback
  • Supportive, close-knit community atmosphere

Standard Groups (7–12 students)

Dynamic classes that combine diversity of ideas with meaningful discussions, perfect for learners who thrive in interactive settings.

  • Broader exchange of perspectives
  • Increased networking and collaboration
  • Efficient, cost-effective learning structure

Workshop Groups (12–20 students)

Practical, high-energy sessions designed for hands-on learning, teamwork, and immediate application of new skills.

  • Project-based, goal-oriented activities
  • Collaborative exercises with peers
  • Real-world application and group feedback
