Profile Photo Guidelines

A great profile photo helps you attract more students

Students are 5x more likely to choose tutors with professional, friendly profile photos.

Do:
  • Use a clear, high-resolution headshot (shoulders up)
  • Smile and maintain eye contact with the camera
  • Use even, natural lighting (face clearly visible)
  • Choose a simple, uncluttered background
  • Wear professional or culturally appropriate attire
Don't:
  • Use group photos or photos with other people
  • Upload blurry, pixelated, or low-quality images
  • Use heavy filters or extreme editing
  • Include hats or sunglasses that obscure your face
  • Use inappropriate or unprofessional content
