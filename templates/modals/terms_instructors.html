Tutor Agreement

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

Last Updated: June 15, 2025

This Tutor Agreement ("Agreement") governs the relationship between Lingocept GmbH ("Company") and individuals ("Tutors") providing language instruction services through the Lingocept® platform ("Platform").

1

Acceptance of Terms

By registering as a Tutor and using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by:

  • This Tutor Agreement
  • The Lingocept® General Terms and Conditions (AGB)
  • The Privacy Policy
  • The Tutor Code of Conduct
2

Tutor Obligations

As a Tutor, you agree to:

  • Maintain complete, accurate, and current profile information
  • Provide instruction in a professional, culturally sensitive manner
  • Adhere to scheduled lesson times and cancellation policies
  • Use only approved Lingocept® communication channels
  • Refrain from soliciting students for services outside the Platform
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
3

Qualifications

You represent and warrant that you possess:

  • The necessary education, skills, and experience to teach your listed subjects
  • Any required certifications or credentials for your offered services
  • Legal eligibility to work as an independent contractor in your jurisdiction

Lingocept reserves the right to request documentation verifying your qualifications and may remove any unverified credentials from your profile.

4

Commission & Payment

  • Tutors earn 70-85% of paid lesson fees, depending on tier level
  • Commission structure may be adjusted with 30 days notice
  • Payments are processed monthly via Stripe or PayPal
  • Trial lessons may be offered at discounted or no compensation
  • All payments are subject to applicable tax withholdings

Detailed payment terms are specified in the Payment Policy available in your Tutor Dashboard.

5

Intellectual Property

License Grant: You grant Lingocept a non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide license to use your lesson content, materials, and communications for Platform operations, quality control, and promotional purposes.

Restrictions: You may not use Lingocept's proprietary teaching methodology, materials, or platform technology for any commercial purpose outside the Platform without express written permission.

6

Code of Conduct

Violations of the Tutor Code of Conduct may result in:

  • Immediate suspension of your account
  • Withholding of payments for completed sessions
  • Permanent termination of your Tutor status
  • Legal action where appropriate

Prohibited Conduct Includes:

  • Discrimination or harassment of any kind
  • Misrepresentation of credentials or experience
  • Sharing of inappropriate or offensive content
  • Circumventing Platform payment systems
  • Violation of student privacy or confidentiality
7

Confidentiality

You agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding:

  • Student personal information and learning data
  • Platform metrics, algorithms, and business processes
  • Internal communications and proprietary methodologies

This obligation continues indefinitely after termination of this Agreement.

8

Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice. Lingocept may terminate immediately for Code of Conduct violations.

Surviving Provisions: Sections 5 (Intellectual Property), 6 (Code of Conduct), 7 (Confidentiality), and 9 (Independent Contractor Status) shall survive termination.

9

Independent Contractor

You acknowledge that:

  • You are an independent contractor, not an employee
  • You are responsible for your own taxes and insurance
  • Lingocept does not provide benefits or worker's compensation
  • You set your own schedule and teaching methods
10

Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Germany, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

The exclusive place of jurisdiction for all disputes arising from this Agreement shall be Berlin, Germany.
